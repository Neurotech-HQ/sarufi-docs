# Billing & Plans

Your **subscription plan** determines what you can do on the platform - how many workspaces you can create, how many chatbots each workspace can have, how large your team can be, and more.

Billing is managed from your **Profile** page: click your avatar at the bottom of the sidebar → **Profile**.

## Your Current Plan

The Profile page shows:
- Your **current plan name**
- Your **plan status** (Active, Cancelled, Expired)
- The **renewal date** (for paid plans)
- A summary of your current usage vs. plan limits

---

## Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to your **Profile** page → **Plans** section.
2. Find the plan you want to upgrade to.
3. Click **Upgrade** (or **Subscribe now**).
4. You are taken to the payment checkout page.
5. Enter your payment details and confirm.

Your new limits take effect immediately after successful payment.

:::tip
Plans are available on both **Monthly** and **Yearly** billing. Choose Yearly to save.
:::

---

## Downgrading Your Plan

:::warning
Downgrading may reduce your limits. Resources you've already created above the lower plan's limits are not deleted automatically - but you may not be able to create new ones until you are within the new limits.
:::

1. Go to your **Profile** page → **Plans** section.
2. Find the plan you want to downgrade to.
3. Click **Downgrade**.
4. Review the **Downgrade Preview** dialog carefully.
5. Click **Confirm Downgrade**.

The downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing period (paid-to-paid) or immediately (paid-to-free).

---

## Cancelling Your Subscription

1. Go to your **Profile** page.
2. In the billing section, click **Cancel Subscription**.
3. Confirm the cancellation.

Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period, then reverts to the **Developer (Free)** plan.

## Plan Limits Reference

When you reach a limit, the relevant **Create** button shows an upgrade prompt.

| Limit | Where it applies |
|---|---|
| `max_workspaces` | Workspaces page - Create Workspace button |
| `max_chatbots_per_workspace` | Chatbots page - Create Chatbot button |
| `max_flows_per_chatbot` | Conversation Flows / Meta Flows - Create Flow button |
| `max_knowledge_bases` | Knowledge Bases page - Create Knowledge Base button |
| `max_team_members` | Settings → Members tab - Invite Member button |
| `mcp_servers_per_chatbot_limit` | Integrations - Add MCP Server button |

:::tip
Hover over the upgrade prompt to see exactly which plan removes the limit you've hit.
:::
